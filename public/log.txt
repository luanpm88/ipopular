'''islamia college''' ({{lang-urاسلامیہ کالج }}) is an educational institution located in the city of [[peshawar]] in the [[khyber pakhtunkhwakhyber-pukhtoonkhwa]] province of [[pakistan]]. it was founded in october 1913 by nawab sir [[sahibzada abdul qayyum]] and sir [[george roos-keppel]] and is considered one of the most prestigious academic institutions in pakistan. the college educates its students in [[art]]s, [[humanities]] and modern [[science]]s. it was a constituent college of the [[university of peshawar]] but in the year 2008 it was given the status of university, with mr. muhammad ajmal khan as its first vice chancellor.
